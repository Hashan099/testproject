Who We Are


At Habarana Kadulla Villa, we are passionate about providing our guests with an exceptional and unforgettable experience. As a team of dedicated hospitality professionals, we strive to create a welcoming and personalized environment where every guest feels valued and cared for.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every aspect of our villa. From the moment you step foot on our premises, you will be greeted with warm smiles and genuine hospitality. We believe in going the extra mile to ensure that your stay with us is nothing short of extraordinary.

With our prime location in the heart of Sri Lanka's cultural triangle, we offer a gateway to some of the country's most iconic and awe-inspiring attractions. We understand the importance of creating lasting memories, and our knowledgeable staff is always ready to provide recommendations and assist you in curating your perfect itinerary.

Our villa is designed to provide a harmonious blend of comfort, elegance, and authenticity. From the beautifully appointed rooms to the tranquil gardens, every detail has been carefully considered to create a peaceful and rejuvenating atmosphere. We take pride in maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness, ensuring that your stay is both comfortable and hygienic.

Food is an integral part of the Sri Lankan experience, and we aim to tantalize your taste buds with our culinary offerings. Our skilled chefs use locally sourced ingredients to create flavorful and diverse menus that showcase the best of Sri Lankan cuisine. From traditional dishes to international favorites, our dining options cater to a variety of palates.

At Habarana Kadulla Villa, we believe that hospitality is not just about providing accommodation; it's about creating lasting connections and cherished memories. Our team is committed to delivering personalized service that exceeds your expectations. We are here to ensure that your stay with us is a truly memorable one, filled with warmth, comfort, and unparalleled experiences.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat, an adventure-filled exploration, or a cultural immersion, we invite you to discover the essence of Sri Lanka with us at Habarana Kadulla Villa. Welcome to our home, where your happiness and satisfaction are our top priorities.
